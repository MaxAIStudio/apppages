ParkTimer Support

Last updated: March 21, 2026

If you need help with ParkTimer, contact us at maxadlaistudio@gmail.com.

Common Topics

0) Settings Overview (Quick Guide)

  • Language: switch between English and Chinese.
  • App color: change the primary app accent color.
  • Save photos to Photos: keep a copy of parking photos in iOS Photos.
  • Advanced: holiday region and parsing-related settings.
  • Reminder times: sound, notification tone, first/second reminder timing.
  • Help: About, Disclaimer, Disclaimer agreement status, Support email.

1) How to Change Language

  • Open Settings in ParkTimer.
  • Tap Language.
  • Select English or 中文.
  • If text does not refresh immediately, close and reopen the app once.

2) How to Change App Color

  • Open Settings > App color.
  • Choose your preferred color.
  • The selected accent color is applied across key UI components.

3) Advanced Settings (Holiday Region)

  • Open Settings and turn Advanced to On.
  • Enable Auto-detect Public Holiday region if you want region switching by location.
  • Or disable auto-detect and manually pick a fixed holiday region/state.
  • If parsing result looks wrong, verify region selection and update holiday data in settings.

4) Reminder Notifications and Sound

If reminders are missing or delayed, please check the following:

  • iOS Settings > Notifications > ParkTimer: allow alerts, sounds, and badges.
  • In ParkTimer Settings, confirm Sound on is enabled and a notification sound is selected.
  • Confirm reminder times (first/second reminder) are set to values before leave-by time.
  • Disable Focus / Do Not Disturb temporarily to verify alert delivery.
  • On simulator, notification behavior may differ. Please verify on a real iPhone.

5) Scan Sign / Photo Recognition

If scan results are inaccurate or empty:

  • Use a clear photo with good lighting and minimal tilt.
  • Capture the full sign/ticket text, including time ranges and exceptions.
  • Avoid motion blur and strong reflections.
  • If needed, retake from a closer distance and ensure text is sharp.
  • Always manually verify leave-by time from the physical sign/ticket.

6) Location Access and Saved Parking Location

If location is unavailable or not saved:

  • iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services: enabled.
  • iOS Settings > ParkTimer > Location: set to While Using the App.
  • Ensure GPS signal is stable (outdoor/open sky is usually better).
  • After granting permission, create or update a parking session again.

7) Pro Lifetime Purchase and Restore Purchase

If purchase status is not updated:

  • Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID used for purchase.
  • Open the Upgrade page and tap Restore Purchase.
  • Check App Store account/payment status on the device.
  • If still locked, contact support with the details listed below.

When Contacting Support

Please include these details so we can help faster:

  • Device model and iOS version (for example, iPhone 13, iOS 18.x).
  • App version and build number.
  • A short description of what happened and what you expected.
  • Screenshots of relevant screens and any error message.
  • For purchase issues: Apple ID region and approximate purchase time.
