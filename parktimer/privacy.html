ParkTimer Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

ParkTimer is developed by MAX AI Studio. We designed ParkTimer to work primarily on your device.

Data Collection

ParkTimer does not collect personal data to our own servers. Parking records, photos, and related app data are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage and Retention

Your parking records and related data remain on your device unless you choose to delete them. If you uninstall the app, locally stored app data may be removed by your operating system. If you enable saving photos to the Photos app, copies stored in Photos are managed by you.

Permissions

  • Camera: Used to scan parking signs and tickets.
  • Photo Library: Used to pick or optionally save parking photos.
  • Location (When In Use): Used to save your parking location and help you navigate back to your car.
  • Notifications: Used to deliver parking reminder alerts.

In-App Purchases

ParkTimer offers a non-consumable purchase (Pro Lifetime). Payments are processed by Apple.

Third-Party Services

ParkTimer may rely on Apple platform services required for app functionality, such as StoreKit (in-app purchase), local notifications, and mapping frameworks. Payment transactions are handled by Apple according to Apple's terms and policies.

Children's Privacy

ParkTimer is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

You can manage app permissions (Camera, Photos, Location, Notifications) at any time in iOS Settings. You can also delete parking records and photos from your device.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact
