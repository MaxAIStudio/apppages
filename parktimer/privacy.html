ParkTimer Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

ParkTimer is developed by MAX AI Studio. We designed ParkTimer to work primarily on your device.

Data Collection

ParkTimer does not collect personal data to our own servers. Parking records, photos, and related app data are stored locally on your device.

Permissions

  • Camera: Used to scan parking signs and tickets.
  • Photo Library: Used to pick or optionally save parking photos.
  • Location (When In Use): Used to save your parking location and help you navigate back to your car.
  • Notifications: Used to deliver parking reminder alerts.

In-App Purchases

ParkTimer offers a non-consumable purchase (Pro Lifetime). Payments are processed by Apple.
