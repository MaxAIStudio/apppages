Common Topics

1) No station plays

  • Check internet connection (Wi-Fi/mobile data).
  • Try another station. Some streams may be offline or geo-blocked.
  • Tap Refresh Stations in app menu to update stations for your selected country.

2) Trial ended and playback locked

  • Open Upgrade to Pro and purchase Lifetime access.
  • If already purchased, tap Restore purchases.
  • Use the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

3) Disclaimer not accepted

  • Open Settings > Data sources, privacy & disclaimer.
  • Review content and tap Agree.
  • Playback is disabled until disclaimer is accepted.

4) Language, color, and country settings

  • Language: switch app language.
  • Color theme: adjust app accent style.
  • Select country: change station region source.

5) Wi‑Fi and mobile data usage

  • Radio playback is streaming and may consume significant mobile data when Wi‑Fi is unavailable.
  • Enable mobile-data warning in app settings if needed.
  • Carrier charges, overage costs, and related losses are the user's responsibility.
  • RadioHubX platform/app is not liable for mobile data charges.

6) Purchase and restore issues

  • Confirm your App Store account is signed in and active.
  • Retry purchase/restore with stable network.
  • If still failing, email us with screenshot and device details.

When Contacting Support

Please include:

  • Device model and iOS version.
  • App version/build.
  • What happened and expected behavior.
  • Screenshots of error or related page.
  • For purchase issues: approximate purchase time and Apple ID region.
