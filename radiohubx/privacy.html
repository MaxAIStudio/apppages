RadioHubX Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

RadioHubX is developed by MAX AI Studio. The app is designed to work without account signup.

Data Collection

RadioHubX does not collect personal data to our own servers. Favorites, recent stations, app settings, and legal consent status are stored locally on your device.

What the App Accesses

  • Network access: to fetch station metadata and play station streams.
  • Audio/background playback: to continue radio playback when app is in background.
  • Connectivity status: optional mobile-data warning logic.

Third-Party Data Sources

Station listings and metadata are sourced from the public Radio Browser directory (api.radio-browser.info). Audio is streamed directly from each station URL. RadioHubX does not host or rebroadcast audio content.

In-App Purchase

RadioHubX offers a non-consumable purchase (Lifetime access). Payments are processed by Apple. RadioHubX does not receive your payment card information.

Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device until deleted by you or removed when the app is uninstalled.

Children's Privacy

RadioHubX is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact maxadlaistudio@gmail.com.
